## Boltobserv

**Source**: https://github.com/boltgolt/boltobserv  
**License**: GPL-3 (GNU General Public License v3)  
**Author**: boltgolt  

### Usage

FragAudit uses radar background images from boltobserv for demo replay visualization. The images are located in `src/radar/boltobserv_maps/`.

### Maps Included

- de_dust2
- de_mirage
- de_ancient
- de_nuke
- de_inferno
- de_overpass
- de_anubis
- de_vertigo
- de_train
