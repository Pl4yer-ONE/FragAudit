# Commercial Use

FragAudit is licensed under **PolyForm Noncommercial 1.0.0**.

Commercial use is **prohibited** without explicit written permission.

---

## What Counts as Commercial Use

Any of the following:

- Selling or licensing FragAudit or derivatives
- Offering FragAudit as a paid service (SaaS, API, coaching service)
- Bundling FragAudit with paid products or subscriptions
- Using FragAudit to generate revenue (ads, sponsorships, etc.)
- Internal business use for profit-generating operations

---

## What Is Allowed

- Personal use (hobby, self-improvement, learning)
- Academic/educational use
- Non-profit organizations
- Contributions back to the project

---

## Requesting Commercial Permission

If you want to use FragAudit commercially:

1. Open a GitHub issue with subject: `[Commercial License Request]`
2. Describe your use case
3. Provide company/project details

Response time: typically 3-5 business days.

---

## Enforcement

Unauthorized commercial use will result in:

1. DMCA takedown notices
2. Legal action if necessary

Don't be that person.
